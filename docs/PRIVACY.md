# Privacy Policy - AI Sales Analyzer

**Last Updated**: November 2024

## Overview

AI Sales Analyzer is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, store, or share any of your sales data.**

### What We Don't Collect
- ❌ Sales data from uploaded files
- ❌ Personal information
- ❌ Customer data
- ❌ Analytics or tracking data
- ❌ Cookies (except essential session cookies)

## Data Processing

### How Your Data is Handled
1. **Upload**: You upload your file through your web browser
2. **Processing**: Data is loaded into your browser's memory (RAM)
3. **Analysis**: All calculations happen in real-time
4. **Display**: Results are shown in your browser
5. **Deletion**: Data is automatically deleted when you close the browser

### Key Points
✅ Data exists **only in your browser's memory**  
✅ **No server storage** - we don't save your files  
✅ **No database** - nothing is permanently stored  
✅ **Session-only** - data disappears when you close the tab

## Third-Party Services

### OpenAI API (Optional)

When you use the AI Assistant feature:

**What is Sent**:
- Your question (e.g., "Which product sold the most?")
- Relevant data snippets needed to answer the question
- NOT your entire dataset

**OpenAI's Usage**:
- Processes your question
- Returns an answer
- May use data to improve their models (per their policy)
- OpenAI Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy

**How to Opt-Out**:
- Simply don't use the AI Assistant feature
- All other features work without OpenAI

### Streamlit Cloud (Hosting)

If deployed on Streamlit Cloud:
- Streamlit may collect basic usage statistics (page views, etc.)
- Your sales data is NOT sent to Streamlit servers
- Streamlit Privacy Policy: https://streamlit.io/privacy-policy

## Security

### Data Protection
✅ **HTTPS Encryption**: All data transmission is encrypted  
✅ **No Permanent Storage**: Data can't be leaked if it's not stored  
✅ **Browser-Only Processing**: Your data never leaves your device  
✅ **No User Accounts**: No passwords to be compromised

### Best Practices
We recommend:
- Use your own OpenAI API key for maximum privacy
- Don't share sensitive customer information in AI questions
- Download reports and delete them after viewing
- Close your browser when done

## Your Rights

### Data Control
You have complete control over your data:
- ✅ Upload and delete at will
- ✅ No data retention
- ✅ No need to request deletion (it's automatic)
- ✅ No data portability issues (you always have your original files)

### No Tracking
We do NOT:
- Track your usage
- Create user profiles
- Sell data to third parties
- Use cookies for advertising
- Collect IP addresses (beyond basic server logs)
